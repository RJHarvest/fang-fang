About Fang Fang

Our Story

CENTRALLY LOCATED IN HONG KONG ALONG WYNDHAM STREET IN LKF TOWER. FANG FANG IS THE ONLY ORIENTAL RESTAURANT LOCATED IN LKF TOWER. FANG FANG IS A SOPHISTICATED AND INTIMATE RESTAURANT, BAR AND LOUNGE WHERE GUESTS CAN ENJOY INNOVATIVE MODERN CHINESE CUISINE AND CREATIVE COCKTAILS THAT TAKE TRADITIONAL DISHES AND DRINKS, ENHANCING THEM WITH A MODERN FLARE AND WITH INFLUENCES FROM THE SURROUNDING REGIONS.

Our Cuisine

FANG FANG IS A HARMONIOUS BLEND OF CHINESE MODERNITY AND TRADITION. TO AWAKEN DINERS’ TASTE BUDS FANG FANG HAS CREATED A POWERFUL CULINARY EXPERIENCE AIMING TO ENRICH CUSTOMERS BY EXPOSING THEM TO A UNIQUE ASIAN FLAVOUR PROFILE.
